How to Reach

By Air :

Flights are available from Delhi to Dehradun (Jolly Grant Airport) thrice in a week. Regular flights are available to Delhi from Mumbai and other metro cities.

By Train :

A range of trains are connected to Dehradun via Delhi.Also many of the rail routes connect Rishikesh and Haridwar via Delhi.

By Road :

Drive Delhi to Haridwar NH-47 (229kms), from Haridwar to Joshimath (257kms)

Transportation from Airport/Train Station/Bus Stand:

Taxis & Private Vehicles are available Delhi to Rishikesh / Haridwar / Joshimath
